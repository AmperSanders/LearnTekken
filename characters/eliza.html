Skills

Eliza

This alluring, blood-sucking vampire was introduced in the Free-to-play installment of Tekken Revolution (2013); but did not become officially rostered until this installment. Eliza is a mysterious character that possess immortality; and yet, keeps her appealing look. Eliza is also a vampire who has narcolepsy; with certain moves causing her to fall asleep. However, this allows her to regenerate health; making her one of two characters with this ability with the other character being Yoshimitsu. She is also one of three 2D characters with Akuma and Geese Howard being the others. She also has an unofficial move that was stumbled across by the community allowing her to transform her divekick into a special, buffered instant divekick; increasing her pressure by a tremendous amount.

Key Moves

1. As mentioned, all of her lows are launch punishable but d+3 is a move that can be special cancelled into qcb or qcf moves; a "1" or "d+1" jab can also be cancelled.

2. A great ranged combo starter that's natural on hit, tailspins, and jails on block. Both hits are high, so they can be ducked if anticipated. She can also perform this move while in Moon Glide.

3. Her most notorious move due to the offensive pressure and execution it takes to perform its instant divekick variation (qcb must be buffered into u/f with a delayed 4). If an instant divekick lands low enough then it gives massive plus frames, allowing for free jabs; which can be cancelled as seen in video #1.

4. A good i11 frame poke that is safe on block and allows for a combo only if the second lands on counter hit.

Notation: d+3, "1", or d+1 special cancels
Notation: f,F+3,4
Notation: qcb+4 (instant divekick: qcb ~ u/f, 4)
Notation: b+2,3
