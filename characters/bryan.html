Bryan Fury

Appearing in every installment since Tekken 3 (1997), this cyborg is the definition of a violent-obsessed fighter. His psychotic, heavy-hitting, brutal playstyle is iconic to the franchise, making him a feared opponent. His aggressiveness can be seen in the many battles against long time rivals such as Yoshimitsu and Lei Wulong. Bryan's offensive nature is what makes him feared, along with the many tools that aid this playstyle. With many tools that counter hit launch the opponent, this leaves Bryan with many opportunities to deal massive damage.
