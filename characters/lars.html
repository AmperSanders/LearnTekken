Lars Alexandersson

An original character from Tekken 6 : Blood Rebellion (2008), Lars was unlike any other character at the time be that he was not only flashy but fast with aggressive tools. It came at a surprise that he was apart of the Mishima family with Kazuya Mishima being his biological father. Lars was and is a peace-seeker that had the goal of end the war between the Mishima Zaibatsu and G Corporation. During such endeavors, he stumbles across the cyborg Alisa Bosconovitch, thus beginning their long journey together.
