Marshall Law

An original character from the first installment of Tekken(1994), Law is the embodiment of the legendary Bruce Lee. Although you can not use his nunchucks in battle, the wide range of iconic moves are at the players' disposal. Known as a great martial artist and restuarant owner, Law can be found funny and charismatic while still remaining a threat in combat. His flashy moveset of flips and slides makes him perfect for putting opponents in constant mixup situations. If you are familiar with the Tekken franchise, you may have come across his Forest Law, his son, in previous installments.

Key Moves

1. A good move used for poking the opponent and gives large plus frames on CH.

2. This move is good for locking down an opponent as it is safe on block and provides a launcher on CH.

3. A really good mixup tool from crouch making your opponent guess between the slide or one of laws WS moves and/or switching sides, it also provides a free wake up on hit.

4. Although risky if the opponent blocks the first hit, this move is still a essential natural-hitting poke.

5. A very essential move to use because it is safe block on gives good frame advantage on hit. On CH, the move gives a knockdown.

6. A important tool to test your opponents throw breaking skills. When not broken, this leads to a combo.

7. Risky due to the last hit being duckable, this tool is still great for whiff punishing and block punishing due to the it's startup frames being i14, as well as being natural on hit.
