Sergei Dragunov

Dragunov made his first appearance in Tekken 5 Dark Resurrection (2005). He is a member of the Russian Spetsnaz whose personality is described as a cold and stoic character that has a very unforgiving and brutal playstyle. Dragunov is regarded as a top tier character because of his tremendous poke pressure and long ranged attacks making him better when played more offensively.

Key Moves

1. A great low poke that is still good despite being nerfed and provides many plus frames on counter hit.

2. A phenomenal move due to its plus frames on block allowing you to pressure opponents heavily; and when learned to do instantly (point blank) becomes even more frightening.

3. A set of moves that can be used to mix up the opponent. The last variation, when used at the wall or balcony, will cause a wallsplat, wall break or balcony break.

4. A good move that launches on normal hit and is safe on block, but can be ducked.

5. This move is good for pressuring opponent when you've conditioned them and allows for a guaranteed stomp when landed.
