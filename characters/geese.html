Skills

Geese Howard

A very strong and prideful fighter, often scorning his opponents, is a guest character from the SNK series Fatal Fury, King of Fighters, and Art of Fighting. He is a 2D character just like the characters Akuma and Eliza. He is often categorized as a very strong character with many pressuring tools to lockdown his opponents; He also has one of the best parry/reversal in the game, which often leads to near death combos if he has meter. However, to fully unlock his potential, the player must have great execution; much like Akuma.

Key Moves

1. A long ranged projectile that is used as a keepout tool and pressure. The double reppuken is two hits when at point blank range but is only used sometimes as a wall ender.

2. One of his best tools when paired with Max mode (1+2+3). It is a hit confirmable, i14 launcher that allows for massive damage and wall carries; and can be used to get a retaliation out of opponents.

3. A good whiff punisher that is a combo starter and is safe on block.

4. A safe homing move that acts as a launcher on counter hit and a tailspin when used in juggles.

5. Another good homing move that launches on counter hit and tailspin within juggles and is safe on block.

6. A great low when used unpredictably on opponents to pressure them low and can launch on counter hit and be cancelled into MAX mode.

7. Being a 2D character, he has access to special cancellable moves into his qcb, qcf, and hcb moves off these initial moves:

  • 2
  • f+1
  • d+1
  • ws+1
  • d+4

8. One of the best parry/reversal in the game. He is able to change the tide of the match in his favor if successful; and even deadlier when meter is present. Thus making your opponent think twice before applying heavy pressure.

Notation: qcf+1 or qcf+2
Notation: b+3,2 (MAX mode cancel)
Notation: f+1+2
Notation: d/f+2
Notation: 4
Notation: d+4 (MAX mode cancel)
Notation: "2", d+1, or "2,f+1" special cancels
Notation: hcb+(1+3/2+4/or 1+4)
