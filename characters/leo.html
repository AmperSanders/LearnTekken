Leo Kliesen

Appearing in the installment of Tekken 6(2007), Leo is a strong, heavy-hitting fighter specializing in the style of Baji Quan. Leo is portrayed as a very strong and humble fighter, which can be seen in the character's cinematic videos/endings. Leo is a character that excels a pretty much everything: good pokes, range, punishment, creating openings, good damage, etc...However, Leo has a very linear playstyle, making Leo susceptible to side-stepping opponents. Leo has a wide range of guaranteed follow-ups from many moves that land on counter hit, making it beneficial to learn what is guaranteed after certain moves.
