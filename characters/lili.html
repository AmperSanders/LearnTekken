Emilie De Rochefort

Emilie De Rochefort, mainly known as Lili, was introduced in Tekken 5 Dark Resurrection (2005) as a character of elegance and maturity, but also a selfish and boastful demeanor. Throughout the installments, she has had a tense rivalry with Asuka due to being defeated by her in tournament. Her fighting style is unique in that it resembles that of a gymnast with tumbling, acrobatic flips and cartwheels being at her disposal. This style allows her to have good movement, okizeme options, range, and much more.

Key Moves

1. A solid move that allows you to chip at your opponents health.

2. A scary, high tracking move that gives advantage on block and hit. This move can be ducked if you're to predictable, but when blocked, you get tons of mixup options allowing you to continue the pressure.

3. A good damaging low that allows you to control positioning and mix the opponent by going backturned by holding back during the animation. At only -12 on block, only 2D characters can launch her. On CH and going backturned, she gets a free 1,2 for extra damage.

4. A good CH mid launcher that's good for checking opponents, locking opponents down by forcing crouch, and offers free mixup opportunities.

5. A fairly long range, mid launcher that is safe on block and leaves her backturned, this move is good for whiff punishing, crushing lows, and may even catch a stepping opponent.
