Alisa

A legacy character making her debut to the franchise in Tekken 6 (2007). Alisa is a unique android-based character with unique features allowing her arms to transform into chainsaws, a detachable head with explosives, and wing-like jetpacks to propel her forward. Alisa is known for her incredible pressure when her chainsaws are summoned and excellent moves to close the distance between her and the opponent. Although she can close distance quickly, she is required a lot of time to be airborne to do so; making her susceptible to being floated out of the air.
