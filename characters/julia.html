Julia Chang

A free-spirited fighter, often depicted as a kind person and a lover of nature, who appeared in Tekken 3 (1997). She was adopted by Michelle Chang at a young age and was taught how to fight. Despite her kind appearance, she is a strong fighter who has many tracking moves to subdue the opponent to her liking. She has great pokes and an excellent wall carry for just about any range.

Key Moves

1. A fantastic move to pressure the opponent and tracks well due to the forward forward input to realign with the opponent.

2. A good tool known as "shotgun" can be used for added pressure as well as creating mixup opportunities. It can also be used to wall carry the opponent with multiple shotgun 1's and shotgun 2 for a for the tailspin.

3. A quick and simple low poke used to chip at the opponent.

4. This grab is unique to Julia because this is one of two i10 grab in the game, meaning this grab comes out the same speed a normal jab.

5. Also unique to Julia is her hopkick. This hopkick is the only hopkick to have a just frame for the second kick. If you do u/f+4,3 with a delay on the "3", Julia will do a plain u/f+4 if the first hit was blocked or whiffed; however if you do u/f+4,3 with a delay and the first kick connects, both hits will be completed for a full launch.

6. Although this move looks like a high, this safe on block mid has the ability to counter hit an opponent and allow for a combo.

7. A risky but rewarding natural low that can be used in Full crouch mixup situations. Although not guaranteed, d+1+2 can hit an opponent if they attempt to get up but can be beaten if the opponent stays grounded or spring attack kick.

Notation: d,D/F+1,(4: spin right, 3: spin left)
Notation: d+3
