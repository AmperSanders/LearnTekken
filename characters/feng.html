Feng Wei

A power hungry martial artist introduced in Tekken 5 (2004) as someone who killed his master due to his impulsive, violent tendencies. He can be a very strong or weak character depending on the player who uses him. He has many aggressive tools to aggravate his opponents and is also capable of dealing with turtling opponents. When he is being pressured himself, he has excellent panic moves and defensive options to get out of intense situations. As suggested, he has tools for players who want to play aggressive and tools for patient players making him a good fit for any situation.

Key Moves

1. A great low poke that has great range and fast startup frames; although unsafe if blocked.

2. Another good low poke that knocks down on counter hit and guarantees a free stomp after crouch cancelling.

3. A deceiving mid attack that gives massive plus frames on hit and is a good checking move. On counter hit, you get a free stomp.

4. A good ws punisher but is best used out of a snakedash because it closes great distances quickly.

5. These moves are a great set of pokes with the d+4 having an extension of 1+2.

6. These set of variations are good for mixing up your opponent. d/b+1,2 is a good mid extension check; d/b+1,4; and d/b+1 ~ F or B to transition into the corresponding stances.

7. A great, safe, two-hit mid poke that is best used at the wall because it causes the opponent to wall bounce upon hitting.

8. Another excellent move at the wall that causes wallsplats and is safe on block.
